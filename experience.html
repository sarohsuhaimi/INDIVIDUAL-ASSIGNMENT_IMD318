◤ My Internship Journey ◢

During my internship, I had the opportunity to work in various capacities that allowed me to expand my skill set, contribute to projects, and gain real-world experience. Below is a summary of my role and key activities I was involved in.

Internship Location: DXN Industries Sdn. Bhd.
Department: Cosmetic, Juice and Household
Duration: 8 weeks (July - September 2024)

Here are the main tasks I was involved in during my internship:

  • Performed data management tasks: Assisted in organizing and maintaining data efficiently.
  • Handled filing duties: Managed physical and digital files to ensure proper documentation and easy retrieval.
  • Maintained training records: Recorded and updated training logs accurately to keep information up-to-date.
  • Managed information effectively: Ensured proper handling, storage, and organization of critical information for smooth operations.

During my internship at DXN, I gained valuable experience in data management, organization, and administrative tasks. I learned how to effectively handle and maintain various types of information, including managing data systems, organizing physical and digital files, and ensuring the accuracy and completeness of training records. This role enhanced my ability to work with attention to detail and reinforced the importance of proper documentation in maintaining smooth operational workflows. Overall, the internship provided me with practical skills in information management, critical thinking, and organizational efficiency, which are essential in any professional setting.
